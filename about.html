Our Story

YAITU ENGINEERING COMPANY LIMITED was established by a team of passionate engineers and construction professionals dedicated to transforming Uganda's infrastructure landscape. Registered with the Uganda Registration Services Bureau (URSB), we bring together expertise, innovation, and commitment to deliver exceptional construction solutions.

  • Registered construction company with URSB
  • Professional team of certified engineers and technicians
  • Specialized in civil engineering and building construction
  • Committed to quality and timely project delivery

Based in Fort Portal Tourism City, Western Uganda, we serve both government and private sector clients across the region. Our mission is to contribute to Uganda's development through quality construction while maintaining the highest standards of safety and environmental responsibility.
